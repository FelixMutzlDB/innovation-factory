CUSTOMER COMPLAINT: TRANS EUROPA - MISSING LOYALTY POINTS
Complaint ID: CMP-2025-00124
Date Received: 14 January 2025
Account: TransEuropa Logistics (FLT-2019-0042)
Status: Resolved

COMPLAINT SUMMARY
TransEuropa fleet manager reported that loyalty points had not been credited to the fleet account for the months of October, November, and December 2024. Total estimated points at risk: approximately 28,000 points (equivalent to 560 EUR at platinum redemption rate).

COMPLAINT DETAILS
- Reporter: Peter Novak, Fleet Manager, TransEuropa Logistics
- Contact: peter.novak@transeuropa-logistics.hu
- Date range affected: October 1 - December 31, 2024
- Volume during period: Approximately 576,000 liters (192,000/month average)
- Expected points: Platinum tier, 2x multiplier - approximately 9,600 points per month

INVESTIGATION
- Loyalty system review: Identified integration fault between fleet card processor and ASM loyalty engine
- Root cause: Software update in September 2024 introduced bug; fleet card transactions not fully syncing to loyalty system
- Scope: Affected all fleet customers using specific card processor batch - 8 fleet accounts total
- TransEuropa: Largest affected account

RESOLUTION
- Points credited: 28,400 points manually credited to TransEuropa account 17 January 2025
- Communication: Personal call from account manager Maria Kovacs to Peter Novak
- Apology: Formal apology letter from B2B director
- System fix: IT deployed patch 16 January; verified for all fleet accounts
- Prevention: Additional monitoring added for loyalty-fleet integration

CUSTOMER RESPONSE
- Peter Novak: Satisfied with resolution
- Relationship: Maintained; no threat to contract
- Feedback: Requested earlier notification when system issues occur - under consideration for process improvement

FOLLOW-UP
- All 8 affected fleet accounts: Points credited by 20 January
- Process: Implement alert for loyalty-fleet sync variance
- Reference: loyalty_program_terms.txt for points crediting policy
