CAMPAIGN: SUMMER PROMOTION 2025
ASM Cockpit Customer Relations
Campaign ID: CMP-2025-SUM | Period: June 1 - August 31, 2025

OVERVIEW
The Summer Promotion 2025 is the flagship loyalty campaign for the peak travel season. Designed to increase fuel volume, drive Fresh Corner traffic, and reward loyal customers during the highest-demand period. Budget approved: 45,000 EUR equivalent in points and discounts.

CAMPAIGN MECHANICS

Fuel - Double Loyalty Points
- Period: June 1 - August 31, 2025
- Mechanic: All fuel purchases earn 2x standard loyalty points
- Eligibility: All ASM Rewards members (Bronze through Platinum)
- Applies to: Gasoline, diesel, LPG, AdBlue
- Exclusions: Fleet customers on custom point arrangements - see contract
- Cap: None - all fuel volume during period qualifies

Fresh Corner Discounts
- Period: June 15 - August 15, 2025 (slightly shorter window)
- Mechanic: 20% off Fresh Corner purchases over 8 EUR for Gold and Platinum members
- Eligibility: Gold, Platinum tiers only
- Applies to: Hot food, cold sandwiches, bakery, selected beverages
- Exclusions: Tobacco, alcohol
- Redemption: Automatic at POS when loyalty card/app presented
- Cap: 2 uses per member per week (prevents abuse)

COMBINED IMPACT
- Members can benefit from both fuel points and Fresh Corner discount
- Target: 15% increase in summer fuel volume vs. 2024
- Fresh Corner: 20% increase in attachment rate during campaign

CHANNELS
- In-station: Posters, pump toppers, shelf talkers
- Digital: ASM app push notifications, email to members
- Social: Paid and organic posts
- Fleet: Email to fleet contacts (fuel points only per contract terms)

BUDGET ALLOCATION
- Fuel points: 28,000 EUR equivalent (estimated points cost)
- Fresh Corner discount: 12,000 EUR (estimated margin impact)
- Production: 3,000 EUR (signage, creative)
- Media: 2,000 EUR (digital amplification)
- Total: 45,000 EUR

MEASUREMENT
- Fuel volume: Compare June-August 2025 to 2024, control for price
- Fresh Corner: Transaction count, attachment rate, basket size
- Points cost: Actual points issued vs. budget
- ROI: Incremental margin vs. campaign cost
- Reporting: Weekly dashboard during campaign; final report September 2025

GOVERNANCE
- Approved: Commercial Director, Marketing Director
- Compliance: Loyalty program terms; no misleading claims
- Contingency: If budget exceeded, fuel points may be capped (unlikely)
