CUSTOMER COMPLAINT: CENTRAL TAXI - INVOICING DISCREPANCY
Complaint ID: CMP-2025-00131
Date Received: 22 January 2025
Account: Central Taxi Group (FLT-2021-0156)
Status: Resolved

COMPLAINT SUMMARY
Central Taxi Group reported discrepancies between driver fueling records and ASM invoices. Specifically, 12 drivers disputed charges on the January 6-12 invoice, claiming they did not fuel at the stated stations or on the stated dates. Total disputed amount: 847 EUR.

COMPLAINT DETAILS
- Reporter: Miklos Balogh, Operations Manager, Central Taxi
- Contact: miklos.balogh@centraltaxi.hu
- Invoice: INV-2025-001456 (January 6-12, 2025)
- Disputed transactions: 12 line items across 8 drivers
- Pattern: All disputes related to HU-BP-007 and HU-BP-015 stations

INVESTIGATION
- Transaction review: Pulled pump and POS data for disputed transactions
- Fleet card logs: Cross-referenced with station transaction records
- Finding: 10 of 12 transactions correctly attributed - driver identification confirmed via fleet card + vehicle ID at pump
- 2 transactions: Found to be duplicate billing - same transaction appeared on two driver allocations due to system error
- Root cause: Allocation algorithm error when driver used backup card; duplicate allocation occurred

RESOLUTION
- Credit issued: 142 EUR for 2 duplicate transactions (credit note CN-2025-0089)
- 10 transactions: Evidence provided to Central Taxi - driver education on fleet card use
- Process fix: Allocation logic updated to prevent duplicate allocation
- Timeline: Credit issued 28 January; evidence for 10 transactions provided 29 January

CUSTOMER RESPONSE
- Miklos Balogh: Accepted resolution
- Request: Improved invoice detail - vehicle number more prominently displayed
- ASM action: Invoice format update in development; expected Q2 2025

PREVENTIVE MEASURES
- Duplicate allocation: System fix deployed
- Invoice clarity: Format improvement planned
- Driver training: Central Taxi to reinforce one card per driver policy
- Reference: fleet_contract_central_taxi.txt for 24/7 invoicing requirements; billing_procedures_b2b.txt
