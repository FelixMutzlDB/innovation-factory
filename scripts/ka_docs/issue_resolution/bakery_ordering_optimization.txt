BAKERY PRODUCT ORDERING GUIDE
ASM Cockpit Service Station Network
Document Version: 1.4 | Based on 2024 Demand Analysis

OVERVIEW
Bakery products contribute 8-12% of Fresh Corner revenue but have short shelf life (1-3 days typical). Over-ordering causes waste; under-ordering loses sales. This guide provides demand-based ordering parameters for ASM stations.

DEMAND PATTERNS BY PRODUCT CATEGORY

Croissants and Pastries
- Peak demand: Morning 06:00-10:00 (70% of daily volume)
- Daily volume factor: Base = 1.0; Monday 0.9, Friday 1.1, Weekend 1.2
- Shelf life: 2 days
- Ordering: Order for next day based on today's sell-through; if sold out before 10:00, increase next order by 20%

Sandwiches and Wraps
- Steady demand: 10:00-18:00
- Daily volume factor: Weekday 1.0, Weekend 0.8
- Shelf life: 1 day (chilled)
- Ordering: Match previous day sales; reduce 15% for Monday (weekend leftover effect)

Bread and Rolls
- Morning and evening peaks
- Daily volume factor: Consistent
- Shelf life: 2-3 days
- Ordering: More stable; weekly pattern; increase Friday for weekend

Cakes and Sweet Items
- Afternoon peak: 14:00-17:00
- Weekend: +30% vs weekday
- Shelf life: 2-3 days
- Ordering: Lower base, spike for weekend

STATION-SPECIFIC ADJUSTMENTS

By Station Type
- Highway: Higher croissant volume (travelers); lower afternoon cakes
- Urban: More consistent throughout day; lunch sandwich peak
- Truck stop: Large format items; bulk rolls; 24-hour demand
- Tourist route: Seasonal; summer +25%; local specialties

By Size
- Small shop (less than 80 sqm): Focus on top 15 SKUs; avoid long tail
- Medium: Full range; optimize mix
- Large: Include premium and experimental items

ORDERING CALENDAR
- Monday: Lower order (weekend leftover)
- Tuesday-Thursday: Standard
- Friday: +15% for weekend
- Saturday-Sunday: Per supplier delivery schedule; some suppliers no Sunday delivery

SUPPLIER LEAD TIME
- Primary supplier: Order by 14:00 for next day delivery
- Backup: 48-hour lead time
- Plan for supplier holidays - advance order

WASTE REDUCTION
- Target waste: Below 6% of bakery cost
- End-of-day: Markdown at 18:00 (20% off) for items expiring next day
- Donation: Partner with local charity for unsold product (where permitted)
- Track: Log waste by SKU; identify consistent over-order items

ASM COCKPIT INTEGRATION
- Historical sales by product available in Cockpit

- Manual override always available for local knowledge
