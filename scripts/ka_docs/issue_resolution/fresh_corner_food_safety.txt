FRESH CORNER FOOD SAFETY PROTOCOLS
ASM Cockpit Service Station Network
Document Version: 2.2 | Compliance: EU Regulation 852/2004

SCOPE
Fresh Corner is the branded convenience food offering at ASM service stations, comprising hot food (grill, hot dogs, baked goods), bakery items, and cold chain products (sandwiches, dairy, chilled beverages). All stations with Fresh Corner must comply with HACCP principles and local food safety authority requirements.

HOT FOOD SAFETY

Temperature Requirements
- Hot holding: Minimum 63 degrees Celsius (145 F) - verify every 2 hours
- Cooking temperatures: Poultry 75C internal, pork 71C, beef 63C
- Reheating: Must reach 75C within 2 hours; do not reheat more than once
- Discard hot food held below 63C for more than 2 hours

Hot Dog Production
- Pre-cooked sausages: Heat to minimum 75C internal before serving
- Hot dog roller: Clean daily; replace water every 4 hours during service
- Maximum hold time on roller: 4 hours from first placement
- Document batch start times on Hot Dog Production Log HD-001

Grill Station
- Clean grill surface between product types to prevent cross-contamination
- No raw and cooked product on same surface simultaneously
- Sanitize utensils every 30 minutes during service

BAKERY ITEMS
- Shelf life: Date label all items; discard after printed expiry
- Ambient display: Maximum 24 hours for cream-filled items
- Storage: Dry, cool area; protect from direct sunlight
- Supplier delivery: Verify temperature of chilled dough products (max 5C on receipt)

COLD CHAIN MANAGEMENT

Receiving Procedures
- Check delivery temperature: Chilled products max 5C, frozen max -18C
- Reject delivery if temperature breach; document on delivery note
- Move to refrigeration within 15 minutes of receipt

Storage Temperatures
- Chilled: 0-5C (refrigerator), verify twice daily
- Frozen: -18C or below, verify daily
- Walk-in cooler: Continuous monitoring; alarm at 7C - see incident_report_cooler_malfunction_2025.txt

Display Coolers
- Maintain 2-5C; do not overstock (blocks airflow)
- FIFO strictly enforced - oldest product at front
- Door seals: Check weekly for damage

CLEANING AND SANITATION
- Food contact surfaces: Clean and sanitize every 4 hours minimum
- Hand washing: Mandatory before handling food, after breaks, after touching face/money
- Glove use: Single-use gloves for ready-to-eat food; change between tasks
- Pest control: Report any sightings immediately; no food storage in non-designated areas

ALLERGEN MANAGEMENT
- Allergen information available at point of sale
- Separate preparation areas for gluten-free where offered
- Cross-contamination prevention: Dedicated utensils, cleaning between allergen-containing products

TEMPERATURE LOGGING
All temperature checks must be recorded in ASM Cockpit or station logbook. Retain records for minimum 6 months. Non-compliance must be reported to station manager and regional food safety coordinator within 24 hours.
