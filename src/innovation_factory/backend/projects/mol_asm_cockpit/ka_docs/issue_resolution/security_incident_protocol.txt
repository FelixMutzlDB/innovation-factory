SECURITY INCIDENT PROTOCOL
ASM Cockpit Service Station Network
Document Version: 2.1 | Classification: Internal Use

DRIVE-OFF PROCEDURES

Definition
Drive-off: Customer leaves without paying for fuel or other products. Includes intentional theft and unintentional (customer forgot).

Immediate Response
1. Do not pursue the vehicle - staff safety is priority
2. Note vehicle registration, make, model, color
3. Note pump number, approximate volume dispensed, product type
4. Note time and any distinguishing features (driver description if safe to observe)
5. If customer returns: Process payment normally; do not confront aggressively

Reporting
- Log in ASM Cockpit within 2 hours: Security > Incident Report > Drive-off
- Attach CCTV footage if available (retain 30 days minimum)
- For amounts over 100 EUR: Notify regional security manager same day
- For amounts over 500 EUR: Consider police report - follow local guidance

Prevention Measures
- Prepay policy: Implement at high drive-off stations (see station-specific guidelines)
- Pump positioning: Ensure clear view of pump from shop
- Lighting: Adequate illumination during night hours
- Signage: "Pay before you leave" at pump islands

THEFT PREVENTION

Shop Theft
- High-value items in locked display or behind counter
- Staff awareness training quarterly
- Suspicious behavior: Observe, do not accuse; report to manager
- Inventory variance: Investigate discrepancies over 50 EUR

Cash Theft
- Cash handling procedures must be followed - see cash_handling_procedures.txt
- Safe drop frequency: Per station risk assessment
- Two-person rule for cash transport where applicable

Internal Theft
- Unusual patterns: Report to regional office
- Audit trail: All voids, refunds, discounts logged
- Anonymous reporting: Ethics hotline available for staff

CCTV MANAGEMENT

Camera Coverage
- Required: Forecourt, pump islands, shop entrance, till area, safe
- Optional: Back office, storage areas per station
- Verify cameras operational at shift start

Footage Retention
- Minimum 30 days for general footage
- Incidents: Retain until investigation closed + 90 days
- Export format: Per system capability; provide to police/insurance when requested

Access to Footage
- Station manager: View live and recent playback
- Regional security: Full access
- Police/authorities: With proper request; document all access
- Never delete footage related to incident

ROBBERY PROTOCOL
- Comply with perpetrator demands - no heroics
- Activate silent alarm if available
- Observe details for later report
- Do not pursue
- Preserve scene for police
- Support staff - offer counseling per policy
