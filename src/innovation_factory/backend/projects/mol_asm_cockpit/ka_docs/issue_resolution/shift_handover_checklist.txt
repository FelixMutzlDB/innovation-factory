SHIFT HANDOVER STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
ASM Cockpit Service Station Network
Document Version: 2.0 | Mandatory for All Stations

PURPOSE
Consistent shift handover ensures continuity of operations, prevents incidents from being overlooked, and supports accountability. Incomplete handovers have contributed to equipment failures, inventory discrepancies, and customer service issues.

HANDOVER TIMING
- Overlap period: Minimum 15 minutes for shift change
- Incoming shift: Arrive 10 minutes before shift start
- Outgoing shift: Remain until handover complete and incoming shift acknowledged

CHECKLIST ITEMS

1. CASH AND PAYMENT
- Till count: Verify cash drawer matches expected
- Safe count: Document safe balance
- Pending transactions: Any authorizations to clear?
- Cash drop log: All drops completed and documented?

2. FUEL OPERATIONS
- Tank levels: Current readings for all tanks
- Out-of-service equipment: Pumps, dispensers, EV chargers
- Pending deliveries: Scheduled for next shift?
- Any fuel quality concerns from previous shift?

3. SHOP AND FRESH CORNER
- Inventory: Low stock items requiring reorder
- Fresh Corner: Hot food batch status, cooler/freezer temps
- Date checks: Items approaching expiry
- Waste log: Documented and disposed

4. EQUIPMENT STATUS
- POS terminals: All operational?
- Coffee machines: Any faults?
- Car wash: Operational status
- HVAC, lighting: Any issues?

5. INCIDENTS AND FOLLOW-UPS
- Drive-offs: Reported?
- Customer complaints: Pending resolution?
- Maintenance: Technician visits scheduled?
- Security: Any concerns?

6. COMMUNICATIONS
- Messages from regional office
- Customer feedback or complaints
- Supplier notifications

DOCUMENTATION
- Complete Shift Handover Form SH-001
- Both shifts sign and date
- Retain in station records 90 days
- Critical items: Log in ASM Cockpit incident module

INCOMING SHIFT RESPONSIBILITIES
- Review handover form before accepting shift
- Verify critical items (e.g., tank levels, safe count)
- Ask clarifying questions - do not assume
- Sign only when satisfied with handover

OUTGOING SHIFT RESPONSIBILITIES
- Complete all checklist items honestly
- Do not omit issues to avoid delay
- Escalate unresolved items to manager if needed
- Leave work area tidy and stocked

MANAGER HANDOVER
When manager changes: Extended handover including staffing, ongoing projects, budget status, audit findings. Minimum 30 minutes overlap.
