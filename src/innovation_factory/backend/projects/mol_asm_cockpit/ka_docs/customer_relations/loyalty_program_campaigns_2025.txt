LOYALTY PROGRAM CAMPAIGNS 2025
ASM Cockpit Customer Relations
Planning Document | Last Updated: February 2025

OVERVIEW
This document outlines active and planned loyalty campaigns for 2025. Campaigns drive engagement, increase visit frequency, and support cross-sell (fuel to shop, shop to Fresh Corner). All campaigns are tracked in ASM Cockpit Campaign module.

ACTIVE CAMPAIGNS (Q1 2025)

Double Points - Fuel (January 15 - February 28)
- Mechanic: 2x points on all fuel purchases for Silver and above
- Target: Increase fuel volume during low season
- Channels: In-station signage, app push, email
- Results: TBD (campaign ongoing)

Coffee Loyalty - Buy 5 Get 1 Free (Ongoing)
- Mechanic: Stamp card or digital tracking; 6th coffee free
- Target: Increase coffee attachment rate
- Reference: campaign_coffee_loyalty.txt for full details

Winter Warm-Up (February 1 - February 28)
- Mechanic: 50 bonus points on Fresh Corner hot food over 5 EUR
- Target: Drive Fresh Corner during cold months
- Eligibility: All members

PLANNED CAMPAIGNS 2025

Spring Refresh (March 15 - April 15)
- Mechanic: 3x points on car wash
- Target: Car wash utilization after winter
- Budget: 15,000 EUR equivalent points

Summer Promo 2025 (June 1 - August 31)
- Mechanic: Double points on fuel; Fresh Corner discounts
- Target: Peak travel season volume
- Reference: campaign_summer_promo_2025.txt for full details
- Budget: 45,000 EUR equivalent

Back to School (September 1 - September 15)
- Mechanic: Bonus points on shop purchases over 15 EUR
- Target: Family travelers, commuters
- Channels: In-station, app

Fleet Onboarding Bonus (Ongoing)
- Mechanic: 500 bonus points for new fleet customers in first month
- Target: New fleet contract activation
- Reference: campaign_fleet_onboarding.txt

Q4 Holiday (November 15 - December 31)
- Mechanic: TBD - under development
- Target: Year-end volume, gift purchases
- Planning: Campaign brief due March 2025

CAMPAIGN GOVERNANCE
- Approval: Marketing and Commercial sign-off required
- Budget: Tracked against annual loyalty budget (280,000 EUR equivalent 2025)
- Measurement: Incremental volume, points cost, ROI
- Compliance: All campaigns comply with loyalty program terms
- Communication: Customer communications reviewed for clarity, compliance
