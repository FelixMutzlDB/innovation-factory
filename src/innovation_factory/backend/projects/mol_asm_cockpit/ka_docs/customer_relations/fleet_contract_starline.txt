ACCOUNT OVERVIEW
Starline Rental Cars operates a large vehicle rental fleet across Hungary, Austria, Croatia, and Slovakia. 500 vehicles (mix of cars, SUVs, vans) fuel at ASM stations throughout the region. Premium tier contract reflecting volume and multi-country scope. Vehicles are customer-driven; fueling patterns vary by location and season.
