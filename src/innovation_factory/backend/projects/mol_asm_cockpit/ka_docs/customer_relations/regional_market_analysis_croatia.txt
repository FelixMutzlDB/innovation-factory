REGIONAL MARKET ANALYSIS: CROATIA
ASM Cockpit Customer Relations
Analysis Period: 2024 | Report Date: January 2025

OVERVIEW
Croatia represents a growth market for ASM with 28 stations primarily along the coast (tourist routes) and in Zagreb. Tourism drives significant seasonal variation. Market is more fragmented than Hungary with stronger independent presence.

MARKET SHARE (2024 Estimate)
- Total fuel market: Approximately 2.1 billion liters annually (retail)
- ASM share: 6.8% (up from 6.2% in 2023)
- Market rank: 5th
- Trend: ASM gaining; expansion in progress

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

INA (Market Leader)
- Strengths: National champion, refinery, largest network
- Weaknesses: Service perception, older stations
- ASM opportunity: Premium experience, cleanliness

Petrol (Slovenian)
- Strengths: Regional brand, highway presence
- Weaknesses: Limited Croatian footprint
- ASM opportunity: Coastal and Zagreb expansion

Crodux, Tifon, independents
- Significant share; price-focused
- ASM opportunity: Quality and consistency

ASM POSITIONING
- Stations: 28; concentration on A1 (Zagreb-Split), coast, Zagreb
- Fresh Corner: 22 stations; key differentiator
- Fleet: Growing; cross-border with Hungary fleet contracts
- Tourism: Summer volume 40% higher than winter
- Reference: HR-C-002, HR-ZG-003 in incident reports

SEASONAL DYNAMICS
- Peak: June-August (tourist season)
- Shoulder: April-May, September-October
- Low: November-March
- Implication: Staffing, inventory, promotions must align
- Reference: seasonal_operations_guide.txt

KEY TRENDS
- Tourism recovery: 2024 above 2019 levels
- EV: Growing; coastal routes need chargers
- Fleet: Hungarian and Austrian fleets fueling in Croatia
- Price: Euro adoption (2023) simplified; competitive

STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS
- Summer preparation: Critical for this market
- Coastal expansion: Evaluate additional locations
- Zagreb: Strengthen urban presence
- Cross-border: Leverage Danubia, Starline for Croatia volume
- Quality: NPS 38 - focus on cleanliness, availability per customer_satisfaction_survey_q4.txt
