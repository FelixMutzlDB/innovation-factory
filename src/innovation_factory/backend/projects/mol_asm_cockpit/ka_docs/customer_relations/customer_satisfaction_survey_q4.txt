CUSTOMER SATISFACTION SURVEY - Q4 2025 RESULTS
ASM Cockpit Customer Relations
Survey Period: October - December 2025 | Report Date: January 2026

OVERVIEW
Quarterly NPS (Net Promoter Score) and satisfaction survey conducted across ASM station network. Sample: 2,400 respondents (800 per month). Mix: 60% fuel-only, 25% fuel+shop, 15% shop-only. Fleet customers surveyed separately (see fleet NPS report).

NPS RESULTS BY REGION

Hungary
- Overall NPS: 42 (up from 38 in Q3)
- Fuel: 45
- Shop: 39
- Fresh Corner: 41
- Car Wash: 35
- Key driver: Price perception improved following Q3 campaign
- Weakness: Wait times at peak (lunch, Friday evening)

Croatia

- Shop: 36

Austria

Slovakia

- Baseline established; compare to Q1 2026

KEY IMPROVEMENT AREAS (Open Response Analysis)

1. Wait Times (mentioned by 28% of detractors)
- Peak periods: Lunch 11:00-13:00, Friday 16:00-19:00
- Recommendation: Additional till during peak; queue management
- Stations: HU-BP-001, HU-BP-007, HR-ZG-003 specifically mentioned

2. Cleanliness (18%)
- Forecourt: Debris, spill residue
- Restrooms: Most frequent complaint
- Recommendation: Increase restroom cleaning frequency; forecourt sweep schedule

3. Product Availability (15%)
- Specific items: Preferred beverage brands, bakery items
- Recommendation: Improve demand forecasting; safety stock for top SKUs
- Reference: inventory_management_best_practices.txt

4. Price (12%)
- Fuel: Always cited; competitive market
- Shop: Some items perceived expensive
- Recommendation: Value messaging; bundle promotions

5. Staff (8% - positive and negative)
- Positive: Friendly, helpful
- Negative: Rushed, unfriendly at busy times
- Recommendation: Staffing during peak; customer service training
